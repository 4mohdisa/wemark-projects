# WEMARK Property Investment Platform

A professional property investment and development platform built for WEMARK, showcasing their strategic approach to land acquisition, house construction, and investor returns across South Australia.

## Business Model

WEMARK specializes in property investment through a proven development model:
- **Land Acquisition**: Strategic purchase of development-ready land in high-growth areas
- **Quality Construction**: Professional project management and construction of premium homes
- **Investor Returns**: Delivering consistent 15-25% returns to investment partners
- **Full-Service Management**: End-to-end project management from acquisition to settlement
